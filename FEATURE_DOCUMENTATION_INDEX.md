## ‚ú® Key Information at a Glance

**Feature Name:** Download Teams (Excel)

**Location:** Team Management page header

**What it does:** Exports all team data to formatted Excel file

**Button Label:** "Download Teams (Excel)"

**File Format:** .xlsx (Excel 2007+)

**Filename:** teams_YYYY-MM-DD.xlsx

**Columns:** 9 (ID, Name, Player1, Player2, Phone, Password, R1Score, R2Score, CreatedAt)

**Styling:** Orange header (#FF6B00), alternating row colors, frozen header

**API Endpoint:** GET /api/teams/admin/export-excel
